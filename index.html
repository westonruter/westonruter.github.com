Thunderboy is a highly intelligent and technologically-inclined elemental of Nordic decent. Thunderboy can control weather and transcommunicate (via electronic voice) through his thundercloud static field. With his static field, he also is able to create lightning bolts and move within them, allowing him to travel at impressive speed. He uses the elements and the forces of nature to help those in need, impressing regular citizens with his dazzling light effects, and also take down the baddest of bad guys. Thunderboy thrills the innocent and puts a real shock into the sinister!
